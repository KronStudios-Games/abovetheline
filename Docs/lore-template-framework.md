
# Settlement Template

## Settlement Name

### Location
- District / Building
- ATL / AL / BTL classification (*See [[Name-Tag Semantics]]*)

### Overview
Short description of the settlement and its general condition.

### Population
Approximate number of inhabitants and demographic composition.

### Governance
Leadership structure and decision-making system.

### Resources
- Food.
- Water.
- Power.
- Medical supplies.
- Trade goods.

### Infrastructure
Description of the structural environment (floors occupied, fortifications, gangway access).

### Environmental Protection
Methods used to avoid or mitigate gas exposure.

### Economy / Trade
Trade relationships with nearby settlements or scavenger groups.

### Threats
Primary dangers faced by the settlement (raiders, structural instability, resource scarcity).

### Notable Individuals
Important characters associated with the settlement.

### Narrative Role
Purpose of the settlement in gameplay or story progression.

# Community Template

## Community Name

### Type
Settlement / Survivor Group / Trade Network / Nomadic Group

### Location
Primary operating area (District / Building / Region).

### Position
ATL / AL / BTL

### Overview
General description of the community and how it survives within the world.

### Population
Approximate size and demographic composition.

### Leadership
Description of governance structure or leadership figures.

### Culture
Shared beliefs, values, traditions, or behavioral norms.

### Economy
Primary means of survival and trade.

**Examples:**
- scavenging.
- crop cultivation.
- equipment repair.
- trading networks.

### Infrastructure
Physical environment used by the community.

**Examples:**
- occupied buildings.
- defensive structures.
- used pathways.
- energy hosting.

### Environmental Protection
Methods used to survive the Cloud and gas exposure.

Examples:
- mask usage.
- sealed corridors.
- elevated structures.
- filtration systems.

### External Relations
Interactions with other groups.

Examples:
- alliances.
- trade agreements.
- conflicts.

### Threats
Major dangers faced by the community.

Examples:
- gas exposure.
- raiders.
- resource shortages.
- structural instability.

### Notable Members
Important individuals belonging to the community.

### Narrative Role
How the community contributes to the story or gameplay progression.
